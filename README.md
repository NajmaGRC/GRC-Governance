# GRC Governance Portfolio
**GRC101 & GRC102 | International Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Academy**

> Governance is where strategy, accountability and control meet. This repository documents GRC framework design, regulatory environment analysis, information security governance, CISO leadership, log analysis, and security integration into business processes — applied across multiple real-world scenarios.

## 📌 What This Repository Contains

Six governance projects covering foundational GRC frameworks, multi-jurisdictional regulatory analysis, information security governance leadership, SIEM log analysis, and security integration into healthcare business processes.

---

## 🗂️ Project 1 — GRC Framework Design: TechStart Innovations

**Files:** `GRC-Frameworks-Principles-Lab.pdf` `TechStart-GRC-Framework-Design.pdf`

Designed a GRC framework for TechStart Innovations — a fast-growing fintech — applying Week 1 foundations. Covered framework selection, risk identification, compliance mapping and governance structure recommendations for a scaling organisation.

**Frameworks applied:** `NIST CSF` `ISO 27001` `COBIT`

---

## 🗂️ Project 2 — Regulatory Environment Analysis: Multi-Jurisdiction

**Files:** `Regulatory-Environment-Standards.pdf` `TechMart-Global-GDPR-Assessment.pdf`

Mapped the regulatory landscape for TechMart Global — a US-based e-commerce platform with operations across Canada, EU, UK and Australia with data centres in the US and Singapore. Identified applicable regulations per jurisdiction and conducted a full GDPR compliance assessment with recommendations.

**Regulations mapped:** `GDPR` `CCPA` `PIPEDA` `UK GDPR` `Australian Privacy Act` `PDPA (Singapore)`

---

## 🗂️ Project 3 — Risk Management Fundamentals: TechStart Solutions

**File:** `TechStart-Risk-Management-Framework.pdf`

Applied a full Risk Management Framework to TechStart Solutions — a 150-person software company facing active cybersecurity incidents including a successful phishing attack, a stolen developer laptop and an unauthorised client environment access. Identified 15 key risks using vulnerability scanning, historical data analysis and threat modelling.

**Delivered:** Risk register with 15 identified risks, likelihood and impact ratings, treatment options and framework recommendations.

---

## 🗂️ Project 4 — Information Security Governance: CISO Role & Real-World Breach

**File:** `InfoSec-Governance-CISO-Roles-Responsibilities.pdf`

Deep dive into the evolving CISO role — from technical function to strategic board-level leadership. Analysed the Safaricom (Kenya) CISO transformation as a positive case study, and examined Kenya's eCitizen platform breach — identifying key risks, cost drivers and three governance recommendations.

**Why this stands out:** Grounded in the Kenyan and East African market context — Safaricom is one of Africa's most significant technology organisations. Understanding governance through this lens is directly relevant to regulated environments in emerging markets.

**Sources:** ISC2, TechTarget, Klogix, NDPC incident data

---

## 🗂️ Project 5 — SIEM Log Analysis: Global Tech

**File:** `SIEM-Log-Analysis-GlobalTech.pdf`

Analysed security logs from a SIEM system for Global Tech — a mid-sized fintech — identifying critical security events, patterns and the top two highest-priority incidents. Each finding is supported by evidence screenshots from the analysis.

**Key findings identified:**
- Unresolved data exfiltration incidents across multiple systems (DB-Server, Webserver, workstations)
- Systematic threat patterns indicating active, uncontained compromise

**Tools and techniques:** SIEM log analysis, pattern recognition, incident prioritisation, evidence-based reporting

---

## 🗂️ Project 6 — Security Integration into Healthcare: VitalBridge Health Solutions

**File:** `Security-Integration-Healthcare-VitalBridge.pdf`

Group project exploring the integration of security into healthcare business processes for VitalBridge Health Solutions — a fictional healthcare organisation. Analysed the challenges of embedding security into clinical and administrative workflows without disrupting care delivery.

**Covered:** Healthcare security challenges, process optimisation, compliance requirements, risk management in regulated health environments.

**Frameworks applied:** `HIPAA` `ISO 27001` `Healthcare security governance`
